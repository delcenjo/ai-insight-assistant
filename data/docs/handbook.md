# Helios Employee Handbook

## Time off

Every employee receives **25 paid vacation days** per year, accrued monthly.
Vacation should be requested at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal
and approved by your manager. Public holidays follow the local calendar and do
not count against vacation days. Sick leave is tracked separately and does not
reduce your vacation allowance.

## Remote work

Helios operates a hybrid model. Employees may work remotely **up to three days
per week**, with core collaboration hours between 10:00 and 16:00 CET. Fully
remote arrangements require manager and HR approval and are reviewed yearly.

## Expenses

Work-related expenses are reimbursed within **30 days** of submission. Upload
receipts to the finance portal. Any travel expense above €200 must be
pre-approved by your manager before booking.
